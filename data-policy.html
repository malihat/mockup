  • What Neighborhoods does RGS Serve? Long Island, the Five boroughs and Westchester County.
  • What are Short Sales? When a bank agrees to release a lien against a property by taking less than is actually owed on the mortgage.
  • What are my closing cost likely to be? The entire closing process is a regulated transaction. You will be given a loan estimate of your fees when you go to obtain a mortgage, which gives an approximation of the total cost of the closing. These figures are estimates, but all of the costs will be fully disclosed and set forth on the Closing Disclosure that must be issued 3 days prior to closing by your lender.
